About Me

Like the ocean, motion design is vast, deep, and constantly in flux. I'm Mares, a motion designer who finds inspiration in the fluidity and rhythm of the sea.

With over 7 years of experience, I specialize in creating animations that flow naturally, capturing the organic movement found in water. Each project is an opportunity to dive deeper, exploring new techniques and pushing creative boundaries.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me freediving, capturing underwater footage, or simply watching the waves - always observing nature's perfect animations.
